Case Study

Pride Create-a-thon

Who: Task Force, Rock the Vote, The Leadership Conference, Microsoft, National LGBTQ Taskforce

What: LGBTQ Create-a-thon

Where: Washington, DC (Mt. Vernon Place United Methodist Church, adjacent to Microsoft Innovation & Policy Center

When: June 13, 2019

How: Aimed at diving into targeted briefs from national and local LGBTQ advocacy organizations, connect with a diverse group of talented people, and collaboratively generate digital media and compelling messages that spread awareness about the 2020 Census to communicate the importance of a complete and accurate count for the LGBTQ community.
